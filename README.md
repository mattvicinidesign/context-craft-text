# Contextual Ipsum

AI-powered placeholder text generator for UI/UX design.

Stop using meaningless lorem ipsum. Generate realistic, context-aware content tailored to your product, scenario, and tone.

---

## What It Does

Contextual Ipsum generates structured UI copy based on your prompt.

Instead of fake filler text, you get usable content like:

- Headers
- Subcopy
- Paragraphs
- CTAs
- Error messages

All tailored to your specific use case.

---

## Example

**Prompt:**

Create issue language for a civic engagement app where users propose political policies

**Output:**

Header:
Require Body Cameras for All Active Duty Officers

Subcopy:
Increase transparency and accountability through mandatory body cam usage

Paragraph:
This proposal ensures all officers are equipped with body cameras during public interactions...

---

## Features

- Prompt-based content generation
- Category-based output (Header, Subcopy, etc.)
- Tone control (Neutral, Persuasive, Formal, Casual)
- Emoji support (optional)
- Multi-language generation
- Random prompt generator
- Regenerate per section or globally

---

## Why This Exists

Designers and product teams need realistic content to:

- Design better interfaces
- Test real scenarios
- Communicate ideas clearly

Lorem ipsum doesn't do that. This does.

---

## How to Use

1. Enter a prompt describing your product or scenario
2. Select tone and categories
3. Click Generate
4. Copy and use the output in your designs
